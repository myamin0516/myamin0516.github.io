Project Overview

This project analyzes median house prices across the U.S. for 2023 to 2024. The data was scraped from realtor.com by Ahmed Shahriar Sakib and last updated in March 2024. It was merged with a comprehensive city dataset that includes county names, providing a clearer picture of housing trends nationwide.

Scope and Goals

The primary goal is to understand house prices around the U.S. This analysis will:

  • Visualize median house prices: Create engaging visualizations that showcase price trends across different states and counties.
  • Identify patterns: Analyze how prices vary based on location.

Conclusion

Coastal and Western houses are pretty expensive.
